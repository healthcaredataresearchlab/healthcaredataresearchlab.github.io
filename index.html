Healthcare Data Research Lab

Healthcare Data Research Lab is the only one in Kazakhstan dealing with the processing of data on the spread of diseases. The head of the laboratory is Professor Abduzhappar Gaipov . This group works as part of the Nazarbayev University School of Medicine in Nur-Sultan. In this laboratory use statistical methods of data processing and machine learning algorithms for predicting the dynamics of diseases.


I am grateful to be supported by the “RESEARCH INTERNSHIP IN WORLD LABORATORIES” COMPETITION of The Shakhmardan Yessenov Science and Education Foundation.

I received my undergraduate degree from the S.Seifullin University with a B.S. in Radioengineering, Electronics and Telecomminications. I also received my MSc degree in State University of Telecomminication named after M.A. Bonch-Bruevich in Saint-Petersburg, Russia.
